The start of a student's university journey marks the beginning of a new chapter in their lives. Starting a new chapter means expanding your opportunities to learn new things. Entering university is a universally shared experience among many students, and many encounter challenges while adapting to the changes. In my one and a half year of university experience, the biggest challenge I had to adapt to was keeping a balance between my studies while keeping myself motivated. In order to keep my life balanced, I started going to the gym. Additionally, it is a blessing that the University of Ottawa is a campus located in downtown. This allowed me to find time and explore the city more. Through the hardships of keeping up my studies, I've discovered that studying at cafes or taking food breaks are a great way of destressing.

Why you should study at cafes
Here is a list of cafes and food places recommendation coming from a university student:
  • Figaro Coffee House
  • Second Cup
  • Cafe Cristal
  • Izakaya Shingen
  • Sugar Marmalade

The list above contains a list of cafes I love studying at and food places I enjoy. These places all possess different atmospheres that have kept my school life balanced. Of all the places I have listed above, I would like to give a special shoutout to Cafe Cristal. The interior of the cafe is cozy and comfortable, giving the perfect atmosphere for studying and relaxing. Their menu also provides a huge variety of drinks and desserts.
