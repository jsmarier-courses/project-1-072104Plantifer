I have recommended up to five places, with each places providing variety of choices. There is an overwhelming amount of orders to choose from, so I will be recommending some of my favorites. To give more context, I enjoy cold drinks like ice tea and anything containing caffeine. My favorite cold drink is Iced Matcha Latte. No matter which cafe I go, I always order matcha. This is a long term obsession I've developped in ninth grade. In terms of food, my favorite is Asian cuisine. I've grown up eating mostly Asian food, which is how I've come to grow very fond of it. One of my favorite restaurants is Izakaya Shingen, a Japanese style restaurant located in Downtown Ottawa. Izakaya Shingen was my main motivator to pass my exams during winter term. The interior of the restaurant looked a lot like Japanese street stalls. The tables and walls were made of wood, there were plenty of Japanese art, a mini bar, and red lanterns. The lighting was dim, creating a cozy atmosphere to destress.

Drinks and food recommendation

Sometimes, I make a bad habit of drinking coffee for weeks straight in order to stay awake. Although I still find myself occasionally doing so, I have decided to resort to matcha. Matcha is a great alternative for coffee, because not only does it contain caffeine, it also contains many antioxidants. Matcha provides many health benefits, which include promoting heart health, liver health, and assists with weight loss.
